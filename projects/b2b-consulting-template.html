B2B Consulting — PowerPoint Template

Service Brand Template
Industry B2B Consulting
Deliverables Full PowerPoint template with master slides, color palette, and typographic system

The Challenge

The client needed a custom PowerPoint template based on their existing website, but had no brand guidelines or style assets in place. They needed something professional, consistent, and easy for their team to use across different types of presentations.

My Approach

I developed a complete visual identity from scratch, including a cohesive color palette, typographic system, and versatile layout elements.

Special focus was placed on clarity, structure, and consistency across slide types, from content slides to roadmaps, timelines, and matrices. Every layout was designed to be both visually polished and practical for the team's day-to-day work.

The Result

A professional, versatile template that the entire team can use with confidence. No more inconsistent decks, just clear, branded presentations every time.
