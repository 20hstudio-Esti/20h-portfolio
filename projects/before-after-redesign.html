Before & After Presentation Slide Redesign

Service Slide Redesign
Industry Corporate
Deliverables 5 illustrated slides from 1 original

The Challenge

The client had a single, text-heavy slide containing five bullet points. The goal was to improve clarity, audience engagement, and visual storytelling, turning dense information into something the audience would actually remember.

My Approach

I transformed each bullet point into its own dedicated slide, creating five illustrated slides that expand and visually explain each idea.

For each new slide I designed a clear layout, added a custom illustration that reflects the topic, and applied the client's brand colors and typography. The result is a sequence that guides the audience through each point with intention and clarity.

The Result

Five focused, visually rich slides that replaced one cluttered page of text. Each idea now has room to breathe, and the audience has a reason to pay attention.
