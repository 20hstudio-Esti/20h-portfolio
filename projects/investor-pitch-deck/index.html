PowerPoint Template & Investor Pitch Deck

Service: Template + Pitch Deck
Industry: Finance
Deliverable: Template & Pitch Deck

Client's Goal

Develop both a reusable company template and a dedicated investor pitch deck. The client needed visual consistency across all their materials, while having a standout presentation specifically crafted for fundraising conversations.

Our Approach

I started by building a flexible template system that serves as the foundation for all company presentations: consistent colors, typography, and layouts that can be adapted to any context. On top of that foundation, I crafted a specialized pitch deck with investor-focused storytelling, clear data visualization, and a narrative structure that takes the audience from problem to solution to opportunity in a compelling way.

The Result

Two deliverables that work together: a template the team can use for everything, and a pitch deck built to perform in investor meetings. Both are consistent, professional, and ready to use.
