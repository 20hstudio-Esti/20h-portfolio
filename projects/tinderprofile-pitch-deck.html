TinderProfile.ai — Investor Pitch Deck

Service Pitch Deck
Industry Tech / AI Startup
Deliverables Investor pitch deck + reusable brand template

The Challenge

The client is a tech startup using AI to improve dating app profiles. They needed both a custom PowerPoint template for ongoing brand consistency and a polished investor pitch deck to present their vision to potential investors.

My Approach

The template ensured brand consistency and ease of use for future presentations, while the pitch deck clearly conveyed the company's vision, business model, and growth strategy.

I focused on clean visuals, engaging storytelling, and modern design aligned with their branding. The deck needed to feel tech-forward and credible while remaining approachable, just like the product itself.

The Result

A pitch deck that tells the startup's story with clarity and confidence, paired with a template that keeps every future presentation on-brand. The founders walked into their investor meeting prepared and polished.
