Modern Corporate Slide Deck Design

Service Slide Redesign
Industry Corporate
Deliverables Two complete presentation decks, dark and light themes

The Challenge

The client needed a versatile, professional presentation deck aligned with modern branding standards, usable across different contexts, from company overviews to client pitches. One design wouldn't fit all their needs.

My Approach

I created two complete versions: a dark background version with bold yellow highlights for high-energy presentations, and a light background version with blue highlights for formal settings.

Both versions include all essential business slides with balanced typography, consistent layouts, and brand-aligned colors. The decks work independently or together, giving the team flexibility without sacrificing consistency.

The Result

A dual-theme system that adapts to any context. Whether the team is presenting at a conference or in a boardroom, they have the right look, professional, modern, and unmistakably on-brand.
