About

I'm Esti.

The designer behind twentyhours studio.
After 3 years designing presentations at a Munich agency,
I went independent. Same quality, my own rhythm.

Now I work focused hours from wherever I am
in the world, designing pitch decks, branded templates,
and presentations that make your message land.

By the time you open your laptop, your slides are ready.

The Name

Why "twentyhours"?

The name comes from working across time zones. While you sleep,
your presentation takes shape. By the time you open your laptop,
your slides are ready.

It's about freedom and focus. I travel, I work from wherever I am,
and I bring fresh perspective to every project. No packed schedule,
no distractions. Just intentional design that makes your message land.
