About

I'm Esti.

The designer behind twentyhours studio. After 3 years designing presentations at a Munich agency, I went independent. Same quality, my own rhythm.

Now I work focused hours from wherever I am in the world, designing pitch decks, branded templates, and presentations that make your message land.

By the time you open your laptop, your slides are ready.

The Name

Why "twentyhours"?

The name is a reflection of how I work. I don't believe in filling every hour of the day with tasks. I believe in focused, intentional work, the kind where every hour counts.

Twenty hours a week of deep, thoughtful design produces better results than sixty hours of distracted output. That's the philosophy: work with intention, not volume.

It also means I have the space to travel, to think, and to bring fresh perspective to every project. Your presentation gets the best of my focus. Not the leftovers of a packed schedule.
